Global Warming

Global warming is the long-term warming of the planet's overall temperature.
Though this warming trend has been going on for a long time,
its pace has significantly increased in the last hundred years due to the burning of fossil fuels.
As the human population has increased, so has the volume of fossil fuels burned.Over time, the cumulative increase
in Earth's average global temperatures is referred to as global warming. It has been said that large-scale
deforestation by humans for various reasons is to blame.

How global Warming destroyes our Earth.


This results in-

  • Extreme temperature

  • Ice melt.

  • Sea levels and ocean acidification.

  • Plants and animals die.

  • Harmful social effects.
